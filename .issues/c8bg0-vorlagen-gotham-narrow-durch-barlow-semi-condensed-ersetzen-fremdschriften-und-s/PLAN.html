Strategy summary

The font swap is mechanical and was verified end-to-end on the live container (one build.py patched, rendered, pdffonts showed three Barlow weights embedded, zero DejaVu). The real risk is baseline + tolerance regeneration — Barlow is narrower than Gotham, so every line-wrap and centering shifts, invalidating the old Gotham-vs-InDesign drift budgets.

  • Foundation first: provision Barlow reproducibly.

    Dockerfile sanity-check on fc-match "Barlow Semi Condensed" + face count, NOT per-weight strings (fc-match resolves those to DejaVu by design; Scribus reads the TTF name table). No fontconfig alias.

  • Swap three layers the naive grep misses.

    16 build.py + 3 *-original.sla PLUS brand.py deffont and shared/ci.yml allow-list. Never edit the generated template.sla.

  • Hard visual gate before any baseline is touched.

    Review all ~72 pages over multiple passes, fix overflow/centering in build.py, re-render — only then promote baselines and re-derive TOLERANCES.yml empirically.

03Decisions

Key decisions & options

One font for all templates

Locked (#42 / CONTEXT 1)

Barlow Semi Condensed only. Weight map Book→Regular, Bold→Bold, Black/Ultra→Black; Minion/Times/Vollkorn→Barlow. Vollkorn is replaced too (explicit user instruction).

Keeping Vollkorn as an italic accent — no local Barlow italic; upright Barlow Black accepted unless review proves a slant essential (Risk R6 checkpoint).

Barlow font provisioning

Chosen

Relocate the 4 OFL TTFs to a committed fonts/barlow-semi-condensed/ and COPY them in the Dockerfile — reproducible from a clean checkout. Deliberate print-pipeline vendoring exception (Scribus has no CDN access). Sanity-check the bare family + face count.

A fontconfig family-alias (like Vollkorn) — verified unnecessary; Scribus resolves natively.

Installing from the gitignored fonts/ drop zone — not reproducible.

Baselines & tolerances

Empirical, post-render

Promote freshly rendered Barlow PDFs to baseline.pdf ONLY after visual sign-off; re-derive TOLERANCES.yml from the actual Barlow drift.

Reusing the old Gotham wrap budgets — they encode the wrong cross-renderer drift and would mis-classify.
