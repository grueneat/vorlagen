Überschriften-Abstände & Falzflyer-Icons

Issue: bx4d8 · Generated 2026-06-07

01Strategy

Four problems, one root cause: stacked single-line frames with per-font baseline offset.

Strategy summary

Multi-line headlines are stacked single-line frames, each placed by an absolute y_mm with Scribus FLOP=1 ("Font Ascent").The visible gap between two lines = Δy_mm − ascent(font_N) + ascent(font_N+1). The old y_mm were tuned so Gotham's ascent gave even gaps; Barlow/Vollkorn ascents differ, so the top gap collapses where a Barlow line sits above a Vollkorn line.
The "dreizeilige" smoking gun is measurable in flyer-a6-querformat-zweigeteilt/build.py:271-303.Frame tops 58.68 / 66.62 / 77.73 mm → gaps 7.94mm (top) vs 11.11mm (bottom). Same font-ascent shift drives the Zeitung Überschrift Dunkelgrün (Problem 2).
Falzflyer social icons are embedded inline RGBA PNGs in ~17.8×15.6mm frames.Two source-fixable causes: the documented Scribus 1.6.x bug that renders white-on-transparent RGBA PNGs invisible under SCALETYPE=1 + high downscale (primitives.py:866-869), or a broken inline blob.
Fix: a metric-driven baseline corrector in the shared emitter, not hand-tuned per-template constants.Given per-line font+size+target LINESP, compute each frame y_mm from real Barlow/Vollkorn ascent (fontTools) so gaps are even by construction. Regenerate the 12 affected templates, re-render under xvfb, pixel-measure, promote baselines only after visual sign-off.

03Decisions

Key decisions & options

Metric-driven shared helper vs. hand-edited per-template y_mmhelper

Hand-editing re-creates exactly the fragile Gotham-tuned constants that broke. A shared corrector reading real font ascent (fontTools) generalizes across the 12 templates and future font changes, and is unit-testable.

Keep stacked single-line frames vs. collapse to one multi-line framekeep stacked

A single frame re-introduces the Scribus mixed-font per-line-leading mis-placement the split was created to avoid. Out of scope for this issue.

Social-icon fix: SCALETYPE=0 + re-packsource fix

Force fit-to-frame to dodge the 1.6.x white-RGBA invisibility bug; re-pack a valid opaque PNG via pack_inline_image if the inline blob is broken. Confirm which cause by rendering one affected falzflyer.

Headline audit: extend line_spacing family, pixel ink-top methodextend E4

Reuse line_spacing_pixel_audit.py's renderer-agnostic ink-top scanner; read SLA geometry via SLADocument. Flag too-tight / uneven gaps, esp. top gap < others. Wire into bin/validate + unittest.
